About CryptoLens

The only intelligence platform focused on the crypto × AI intersection.

What We Do

CryptoLens monitors 11+ sources across crypto media, AI research, and on-chain data. Every 2 hours, our pipeline fetches new content, scores it on a 1–10 importance scale using Gemini AI, and surfaces only the stories that matter (score ≥ 7).

We don't just aggregate — we filter. The average score across all fetched content is around 5. You only see what scored 7 or above. Editor's Take provides context on why a story matters, not just what it says.

The Crypto × AI Angle

Most crypto media covers markets. Most AI media covers models. Nobody covers the intersection: AI agents on-chain, tokenized compute markets, AI-driven trading protocols, regulation touching both sectors. That's our focus.
